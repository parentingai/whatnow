Frequently asked

The wheel keeps showing the same activities.
The app remembers your recent results and tries to avoid repeating them. If your filters narrow the pool too far, there isn’t much variety left to show. Try widening the filters, or open Customize Wheel to see how many activities are in your current mix.

How do I add my own activity?
Tap Customize Wheel from the home screen. You can toggle existing activities on and off, and any custom ones you add stay on your device.

Does the app need internet?
No. The activities and the spinner work fully offline. The app only contacts the network to send anonymous crash reports and usage counts.

Is my data safe?
The app does not collect names, accounts, or anything personal. See the full Privacy Policy.
