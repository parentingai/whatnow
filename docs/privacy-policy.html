Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Overview

WhatNow? Kids is an activity-spinner app that helps parents and caregivers decide what to do with their kids. It is built and operated by Parenting AI.

Data Collection

The app collects two narrow categories of anonymous data: usage events (app opened, spin completed, customize opened, empty filter combinations) and crash and performance data. We do not collect names, emails, accounts, location, contacts, photos, or any device advertising identifier.

Local Storage

Custom activities, excluded items, and recently-shown activities are stored only on your device and are never transmitted off it.

Analytics & Tracking

Anonymous usage and crash metrics are collected via Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve functionality and fix bugs. We do not use the Apple advertising identifier (IDFA), do not show ads, and do not track you across other apps or websites. Firebase’s own privacy notice: firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. No other third-party services or APIs are contacted.

Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for parents and caregivers; no personal information is collected from anyone, including children. Custom activities you add stay on your device.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal information, there is no profile to access, correct, or delete. Deleting the app removes the anonymous Firebase installation ID tied to your device.

Changes

Policy updates appear on this page with a revised effective date.
